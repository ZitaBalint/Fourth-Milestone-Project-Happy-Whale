Our Story

Established in 2018 and run by identical twins Emily and Victoria Bradley. Happy Whale is a small UK business which specialises in children's clothing aged 0-6 years. We have designed a range of comfortable, easy wear clothing that is practical and perfect for your little ones as they grow. Having worked as nannies for over 10 years we understand the need for durable clothes that are also stylish too. We use only the highest quality fabrics which are incredibly kind and comfortable for babies and toddlers. All of our clothing is machine washable.
